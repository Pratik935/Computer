Multimedia

Multimedia refers to the integration of various forms of media,
including text, audio, images, animation, video, and interactive
content, to convey information or create experiences. It involves
combining multiple forms of content into a single medium,
typically delivered through computers,mobile devices, and the internet.

Key Components of Multimedia
  1. Text

    2. The most basic form of media, often used to convey information,
    instructions, or context. Text is typically combined with other
    elements, like images or video, to enhance communication.


  2. Audio

    3. These are used to enhance the experience or to convey actions or emotions.




  3. Video

    4. Moving images or video clips, which can be used for storytelling,
    tutorials, demonstrations, or entertainment. Video often includes
    both visual and audio elements to convey meaning.




  4. Image

    5. Still images such as photographs, illustrations, or graphics.
    These are used to visually represent concepts, ideas, or emotions.




  5. Animation

    6. Animated graphics or characters that bring a sense of motion
    to otherwise static images. Animation can be 2D or 3D and is widely
    used in entertainment, education, and advertising.
